<h1>ISBAR</h1>  
- introduction - introduce self and your role  
- situation - vital signs  
- background - medical history, date of admission, diagnosis, current and previous (shift) treatments / procedures  
- assessment - vital signs; inspection, auscultation, percussion, palpation; recent changes to baseline  
- recommendation - ask for any order changes and what you think would be helpful
