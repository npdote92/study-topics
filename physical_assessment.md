# Physical Assessment

## ABCDE
- airway
- breathing
- circulation - blood pressure
- disabilities
- exposure - presentation of patient

## preparation for physical exam
- infection prevention - PPE
- environment - privacy, minimal distraction
- equipment
- patient preparation - explain procedures, make patient comfortable
## head toe assessment
- general survey - apparent state of health, level of consciousness, signs of distress
- vital signs
- integumentary - skin, hair, scalp, nails
- head and neck
- eyes, ears
- nose and sinuses
- mouth and pharynx
	- buccal mucosa (lining of cheeks and lips), gums, teeth
	- tongue
- throat - pharynx, neck
- chest
	- heart
	- breast
- torso / extremities
	- deep tendon reflex
- abdomen
	- auscultate first before palpation or percussion
- genitalia
- rectum, anus

## physical assessment techniques
- inspection - visual
- palpation - tactile / touch; use of hands
- percussion - striking or tapping - percussion note / sound
- auscultation - hearing; usage of stethoscope
